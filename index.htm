WHAT IS AI

AI is a broad field within computer science focoused on creating systems teht can perform tasks that typically require human intilligence. This includes:

  • Learning:

    • AI systems can learn from data,identity patterns, and improve their performance over time.

  • Reasoning:

    • AI can analyze information and draw conclusions based on the data it has been trained on.

  • Problem-solving:

    • AI can be used to fined solutions to complex problems by analying data and identifying optimal paths.

  • Perception:

    • AI can enable machines to "see" an dunderstand the world around them through computer vision.

  • Language processing:

    • AI can enable machines to understand, interpret, and generate human langauage through"natural language processing"(NPL)
